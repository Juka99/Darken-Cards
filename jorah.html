Story of Jorah

Jorah is a royal general of Landor, city of light. His family rules the Landor. Jorah and his sister Lorena were raised in a bonton manner. They were properly thought how to fight and defend themselves. They were also educated on academy of Landor. They were thought to always serve and fight for their kingdom and people in it. Jorah became an army general on age of 25. He is one of the best generals in the whole Regnora. He defended city many times, and conquered many cities and kingdoms. People love him for his humbleness and empathy for others. Jorah never failed in his missions, and that's how he received a nickname ,,The Glorious One,,. As the years went on, Jorah's sister, Lorena became very bitter and evil. She tried to posion her own father in order to take the throne. But nothing gets past Jorah's eyes. So in a middle of celebration, Jorah threw the poisonous wine cup out of his fathers hands and revelead what Lorena intented to do. He captured her and by orders of his father, he expelled her from the kingdom forever.
