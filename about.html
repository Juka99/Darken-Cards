Who are we ?

Hello there friend ! Welcome to Darken Cards Inc. We are a team of handfull of people with similiar mindset with only one goal in our minds. To make the best card game ever ! We constantly work on new cards and we are always trying to get fresh content out and make the game as exciting and fair as possible !

A little about us

We come from Belgrade, Serbia. We always had a huge passion for cards, especially magic cards games, like Yu-Gi-Oh. We finished Faculty of ICT and became Web Developers, so we decided to make our child dream come true and make an awesome cards game with all the knowledge we gathered over the years !

Final goal

I guess what we are trying to show you guys here is that cards will never die as a game. Never. Our goal is to be best in the world, and we are ready to wok hard for it ! So go ahead and contact us, tell us what you think. We would love to hear your feedback !
