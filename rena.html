Story of Rena and Mark

Rena and Mark fell in love when they were just a kids. Both from very powerful families, they were always together. They grow up in city of Blust, city of deception and snakes. They attended same classes in school, and did everything together. As years went on, their love was bigger by the day. They wanted to be together forever. They made a pact with Solon, the snake lord. They will kill his enemies worship him, and in return he will make them immortal. But, what they did not know that great evil consumes you when you accept the power of the snake. They both became very evil, and swore to only care about each other and nobody else. Once they found out how powerful they are, they confronted and killed Solon himself, becoming the new snake lords.
