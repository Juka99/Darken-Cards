Story of Nora

Nora comes from city of Noc, city of shadows. Nora lost everything at a very young age. Her parents were murdered by bandits and she was sent to foster care. On her 11th birthday, a family came in foster center and adopted Nora. What she found out soon that it was a family of assassin's. They thought Nora everything she knows, they made her a shadow in the night. Nora's main task is to kill high-leveled corupted people in order to find out who ordered death of her parents. It is said that she herself killed Levion when he was just a man. Some people blame her for starting the apocalypse of Levion. She now wonders the lands of Regnora, traveling only in the vail of night, looking for answers. And she will destroy anyone in her way.
