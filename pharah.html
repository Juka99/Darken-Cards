Story of Pharah

Pharah is a princess of Ionia. She was just a kid when Ionian banished Andros. Her parents were king and queen of Ionia. They were very strict and always bussy. They thought Pharah to always be bitter and never connect with anyone. They paid some of the best assassins in Regnora to come and teach her to kill. Thanks to that, Pharah became best Ionian assassin, killing diplomats and enemy generals in the name of Ionia. When she was 19, Ionia was attacked by Decentents, assassins from city of Shor. In the vail of night, they started killing Ionian soldiers and generals from stealth. They were spotted by an Ionian soldier and the alarm was triggered. Everyone was on their feet. Pharah was in her room when she heard the noise. She ran outside to see the worst picture in her life. Her mother and father were assassinated amongst other generals and soldiers. In a consumed hatred, Pharah found and confronted the assassins before they left the city. She killed them all single-handedly and came back a different person. From that day, she became a cruel Queen of Ionia, in a quest to conquer all of Regnora.
