Story of Levion

Levion was born in city of Winterfell, but he left it at a very young age cause his parents where international traders, so they moved a lot. He was raised to be just and hard-working young man, he was always following the rules. When his parents died he started traveling the lands of Regnora to continue his family bussiness. But one day, when he arrived in city of Tartar, darken city, he encountered a strange blue demon. Demon said that he was captured in the city for 9 eons because he tried to destroy whole Regnora. He wanted to make a pact with Levion. He asked for his soul when he dies, and in return, he will make Levion immortal after he comes back to life. Levion refused and ran away in fear. 2 years later Levion found himself trading goods in Light Bay, the biggest trading market in Regnora. On that day, he lost everything. When night came, he started going back to the little tavern nearby. On his was there he saw strange shadow on the roof. And in a blink of an eye, shadow appeared in front of his eyes, pushing him to the wall. Levion couldn't even open his mouth from fear. As the moonlight lightened the little street, shadowy figure started to reveal its self. It was Nora. Levion begged her not harm his, cause he is just a trader. Nora started laughing and revelead to him that he is a son of royals. His parents were never traders, they were people with very high influence and they always had to travel accross the continent so they could make new bussinesess, but they always wanted to keep their son protected. She revelead to him that she is the one that killed his parents and was searching to kill him from that day since. Before Levion even got a chance to say anything, Nora stabbed him right in the heart, leaving him to bleed in a dark alley. On his last breath, he found himself between life and death. The blue demon appeared in a strange fog. He offered Levion the pact once again. ,,Let us destroy them all together, frined,, he said to Levion. In hatred of what he found out, Levion accepted his offer. Blue demon smiled. Dark lightning fell from the sky and hit Levion's dead body. Levion was resurected, but he didnt look like himself. His skin became blue, he would freeze anything he touched. The power he felt was out of this world. From that day, Levion's only quest is to destroy all of Regnora and kill Nora for what she has done to him.
