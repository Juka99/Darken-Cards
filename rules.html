Rules of the game

Every player starts chooses his champion and 2 allies. Every third turn every player chooses 1 additional ally champion. Any card can attack any card. If attack of the attacker is bigger then defense of champion that is being attacked, it wins the battle. Vice versa, the attacked champion has a chance to deflect the attack. Every player throws a dice each turn. Who gets the bigger number gets to power up 1 of their champions for 1 turn. The more stamina champion has, the bigger are chances that he will deal double damage on attack. The higher the defense percentage is, the higher is a chance of champion staying in the game. If both players get number 6 on a dice role, they can both power 1 champion of their choosing. The first player that destroys enemy main champion wins. Let the game begin !
