Orwen

The Trickster

Orwen's story

Orwen come from Aughard, city of magic and and dark arts. Orwen's parents were lunatics that used their magic to torture Orwen when he didn't obey them. Orwen was broken. One day when his parents sent him outside the gates to shop he encountered a strange looking creature. It was Levion. Levion saw a great potential in a little boy and offered him his help. Orwen accepted and told Levion what his parents do to him every day. Levion smiled and gave him a strange glowing stone. He told him to take it. Once Orwen touched the stone, his full potential was unlished. Orwen killed everyone in his city, and Levion revived them as his servents. From that day, Orwen is Levion's right hand, an unstoppable monster that uses magic and deception to carry his tasks and kill.

Specialty : Magic
