Story of Lagos

Lagos was raised in city of Sandor, city of gates and keeps. His family were known as ,,keepers,,. Every member in his family had this position. So does Lagos. His parents dedicated their whole lives teaching Lagos ways of justice, morality and self-worth. Lagos become keeper of Ragnar's tomb. Legend says that deep in the tomb, there is a dark shard that grants unthinkable power. Shard's dark powers were so big that even Lagos got tempted to take it, but thanks to his iron will, he broke the spell and became the master of tomb. For eons people are trying to find a shard in a lust for power. But anyone who ever tried to get in the tomb and confronted Lagos were never seen again. Lagos is always somewhere near the tomb, scouting for people that are trying to get in.
